## What the app is for

The app is where you connect a wallet, authenticate private state, shield supported stablecoins, execute private payouts, review activity in Parly Ledger & Compliance, prepare a scoped receipt, and hand that receipt to Verify when proof is needed.

## Connect and authenticate

Parly does not treat wallet connection and private-state access as the same thing.

The user flow is:

1. connect your wallet
2. authenticate by signing the fixed auth message
3. recover private state from indexed chain data
4. enter the ready state with shielded balance and execution controls unlocked

This is why a connected wallet is not automatically a ready private session.

## Shield supported stablecoins

Parly supports shielding `USDC` and `USDT`.

You can shield:

- directly on Tempo
- from Ethereum into Tempo
- from Arbitrum into Tempo
- from Base into Tempo
- from BSC into Tempo

The app should keep finality honest. If a source-side action lands but Tempo settlement is still unresolved, the UI should show recovery or uncertain-finality state instead of pretending the result is already final.

## Execute private payouts

The app supports:

- private same-chain payouts on Tempo
- private cross-chain stablecoin-only payouts from Tempo to supported spokes
- single-send execution
- batch execution up to `10` outputs per proof
- third-party private relay over Waku by default
- self-relay fallback when needed

The app should keep `submitted`, `pending`, `confirmed`, and `failed` distinct.

## Review in Parly Ledger & Compliance

Parly Ledger & Compliance is the in-app review surface for deposits, private executions, change-note activity, scoped receipt preparation, and proof handoff.

You should use it to:

- review deposit history
- review private execution history
- open one payout scope only
- export or share the data needed for one selective verification flow

## Hand off to Verify

Verify works from two values:

- the confirmed Tempo transaction hash
- the disclosed child key

The app should help you prepare that narrow disclosure cleanly, then hand the reviewer off to the public Verify portal.

## Boundaries to keep in mind

The app does not turn Parly into:

- a scheduler
- a swap router
- a volatile-token payout system
- a passkey privacy product
- a protocol-owned exclusive relay network

It is a private stablecoin execution surface with deterministic recovery and selective proof.
